About Lecture

General Relativity (I)

Time is an illusion. - Albert Einstein

Aim of this course (teaching in English) : We will cover the idea of spacetime and Einstein’s special and general theory of relativity, emphasizing the physical intuitions.

  • time: Monday 13:20 - 16:20 (18 weeks; calendar)

  • location: F102

  • scoring: homework (30%) + mid-term exam (30%; week 9) + final exam (30%; week 18) + class involvement (5%) + others (5%)

  • TA: Hsiang-Yu Huang (siangyu1016_at_gate.sinica.edu.tw)

  • main textbook: "Gravity: An Introduction to Einstein's General Relativity" by James B. Hartle (available in NTNU library; reserved for this course)

  • online references:

  1. book list of relativity (copyright)
  2. The Genesis and Renaissance of General Relativity (youtube video, public lecture by Jurgen Renn)
  3. Einstein for everyone (course website by Prof. John D. Norton at University of Pittsburgh)
